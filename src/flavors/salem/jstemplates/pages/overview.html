About

We want our sidewalks and streets to be safe, so that children and families can walk or bike around their neighborhoods, to school, and to play areas. Please help us by identifying unsafe conditions on a route that you use and giving us suggestions about how to improve them.

This information will be shared with the principals and parents associations of each school, relevant neighborhood associations, city organizations, and city departments as applicable to assist them with their own planning and safety initiatives.

Travel survey!

If you have school age children please go here and fill out this quick survey on how they get to school. It is completely anonymous and will help inform further analysis of the data collected on the map.

Privacy and your information

Your participation in this project is voluntary and is appreciated. You may choose to share your name, email address, and mode of travel to school.

If you share your name, we will display it on the website alongside your contributions to the map. We suggest sharing your first name only, since this is a public website.

If you share your email address, it will only be used by the Salem Mass in Motion Coordinator to provide you with further information about the project. Your email address will not be visible on the map. It will not be shared with principals or parents associations.

If you share your model of travel to school, it will only be used by the Salem Mass in Motion Coordinator to better understand how information on the map relates to travel patterns. It will not be visible on the map.

Please don't share your home address or any identifying information on the map.
